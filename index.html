Hi, I'm Aravind Machiry (a.k.a donfos) and I want to make smartphones the most secure devices.

Prof. Christopher Kruegel and Prof. Giovanni Vigna co-advise me towards my goal. I am a Third year PhD student at University of California, Santa Barbara. Currently, I am working on applying static/dynamic program analysis and fuzzing to improve the security of various components (Operating system, Trusted Execution Environment, etc) of the Smartphone. I am also interested in identifying and improving the weaknesses of static program analysis.

If you want to know more about my research, discuss problems related to the system security or program analysis, or need a workout partner for your CrossFit WoD, do not hesitate to contact me :)
